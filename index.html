Questions

Straightforward answers to what clients most frequently ask.

What types of legal matters do you handle? +
Primarily corporate law, contract drafting & compliance, civil & property law, and constitutional matters. This includes drafting agreements, legal notices, plaints, affidavits, and providing research-backed legal advisory.
Do you offer online or remote consultations? +
Yes. Consultations are available both in-person (Vadgaon, Pune) and remotely via video call or phone, making it convenient for clients across Maharashtra and beyond.
Can you draft contracts and legal agreements? +
Absolutely. Contract drafting is a core service — covering service agreements, partnership deeds, NDAs, employment contracts, rental agreements, and custom commercial contracts tailored to your requirements.
How do I book an initial consultation? +
Use the contact form below, send an email to advocate.snehalmore@gmail.com, or reach out via WhatsApp. Initial consultations are focused on understanding your matter and outlining the best approach forward.
Which languages can you communicate in? +
English, Marathi, and Hindi — ensuring clear and comfortable communication for clients from across Maharashtra and other regions of India.
